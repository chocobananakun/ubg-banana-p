Getaway Shootout is a chaotic and hilarious multiplayer shootout game where players race to escape while battling each other with various weapons. It's a physics-based platformer with ragdoll characters and intense action.

How To Play

This 2 player game created double the fun when you team up with a friend using the same keyboard. Don't have a partner to play with? The game is available in one-player mode. Choose one of the characters at the beginning of the game and jump right in.

Player 1 Controls W to jump left E to jump right R to use a power-up Player 2 Controls I to jump left O to jump right P to use a power-up
