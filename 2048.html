Vex 8 unblocked

Tag Unblocked is a multiplayer online game where players engage in an exciting chase within a 2D environment. One player is chosen as the tagger and must try to touch other players to "tag" them. When a player is tagged, they become the new tagger.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Use checkpoints wisely and practice wall jumps. Study trap patterns before making difficult jumps. Look for hidden paths that might contain secret collectibles.
