Penguin Diner Unblocked

Penguin Diner is a simulation game where players manage a diner and serve customers as a penguin. The goal is to earn money, upgrade the diner, and keep the customers happy.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Serve customers quickly and efficiently to earn more money and keep them happy. Upgrade your diner to accommodate more customers and improve service. Learn the preferences of different customer types.

Use MOUSE to play the game.
