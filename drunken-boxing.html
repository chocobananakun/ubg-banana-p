Drunken Boxing Unblocked

Drunken Boxing is a hilarious and chaotic fighting game where players control wobbly fighters with exaggerated movements, attempting to knock each other out with clumsy punches and kicks.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Learn to control the wobbly movements to land effective punches. Time your attacks carefully to exploit your opponent's unsteady stance. Use the environment to your advantage. Experiment with different fighting styles to find what works best. Practice to improve your coordination and prediction skills. Have fun and embrace the chaos!

PLAYER 1: "ARROW KEYS" PLAYER 2: "W,A,S,D" You can use the left and right sides on touch screens.
