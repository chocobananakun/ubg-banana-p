Fireboy and Watergirl 2 in The Light Temple Unblocked

Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple is a cooperative puzzle-platform game where players control two characters, Fireboy and Watergirl, to navigate through a series of challenging levels within a light-themed temple. Utilizing beams of light, mirrors, and other light-based mechanics, they must work together to solve puzzles and reach the exit.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Coordinate Fireboy and Watergirl's movements and utilize the properties of light to solve puzzles. Fireboy can traverse lava, and Watergirl can traverse water. Manipulate light beams with mirrors and prisms to activate mechanisms and open pathways. Collect diamonds of their respective colors to unlock doors. Teamwork is essential to overcome the challenges in the Light Temple.

Move with WASD and the arrow keys.On mobile you can change controls in the options menu.
