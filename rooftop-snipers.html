Rooftop Snipers Unblocked

Rooftop Snipers is a hilarious and competitive 2-player game where you battle it out on rooftops, trying to knock your opponent off with well-timed shots. It's a physics-based game with comical ragdoll animations and intense duels.

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Whether you play against the computer in 1 Player mode or compete with a friend in 2 Player mode; this game, controlled with just two buttons, will test your reflexes and timing.

The game is controlled with just two buttons. You jump with "W", raise your gun with "E" and shoot. In 2 Player mode, the second player uses the "I" and "O" keys for the same functions. Aim and shoot at the right time to push your opponent off the roof. Control the recoil force while shooting, you may fall if you lose your balance. It is important to jump at the same time as your opponent, make sudden moves and catch the right moment. Have a different experience in each match by fighting with various weapons. Make more shots, keep your balance and win the duel by knocking your opponent down!
