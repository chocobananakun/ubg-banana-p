How To Play

Aim carefully and consider the trajectory of your arrow. The ragdoll physics can lead to unexpected and hilarious outcomes. Experiment with different angles and power to hit your targets effectively. Some levels may require hitting specific body parts or achieving multiple hits.

Control the archer using the mouse or by touching the screen on the left highlighted part of the screen. Hold the mouse (finger) behind the archer's back to draw the bow (no need to pull it horizontally). Keeping the bow drawn, aim and release.
