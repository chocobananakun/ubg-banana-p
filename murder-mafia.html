Murder Mafia Unblocked

Murder Mafia is a social deduction game where players are secretly divided into two teams: the Mafia (who know each other) and the Townspeople (who don't). The Mafia's goal is to eliminate all Townspeople, while the Townspeople must identify and eliminate all Mafia members. Gameplay involves discussion, voting, and sometimes special roles with unique abilities.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Pay close attention to player behavior and statements. Formulate logical deductions based on the available information. Be persuasive when defending yourself and accusing others. If you are Mafia, coordinate your eliminations secretly. If you have a special role, use your ability strategically to help your team.
