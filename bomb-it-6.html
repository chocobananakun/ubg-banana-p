Bomb It 6 is a classic Bomberman-style arcade game where players navigate mazes, strategically place bombs to destroy crates and enemies, and collect power-ups to become the ultimate bomber.

Core Features

  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Enhanced ragdoll physics
  • Wall-jumping mechanics
  • Underwater segments
  • Custom level editor
  • Free to play

How To Play

Strategically place bombs to clear paths and defeat enemies without getting caught in the explosions. Collect power-ups to enhance your bombing abilities. In multiplayer mode, outsmart your opponent and be the last bomber standing. Pay attention to the blast radius and the timer of your bombs.

1st Player: Move: "W, A, S, D" Bomb: "SPACE-BAR" 2nd Player: Move: "ARROW KEYS" Bomb: "ENTER"
