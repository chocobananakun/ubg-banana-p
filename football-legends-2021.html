How To Play

Time your shots carefully and utilize the special abilities of your chosen legend to gain an advantage. In multiplayer mode, coordinate your movements and passes to outmaneuver your opponent and score more goals. Pay attention to the power gauge for special shots.

If playing single: Move: "ARROW KEY" or "W,A,S,D" Shot: "X" or "L" Supershot: "Z" or "K" If playing two players: Player 1: Move: "W,A,S,D" Shot: "B" Supershot: "V" Player 2: Move: "ARROW KEY" Shot: "L" Supershot: "K"
