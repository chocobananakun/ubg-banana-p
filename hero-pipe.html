Hero Pipe is an addictive puzzle game where players must use their imagination to connect pipes correctly to guide water, lava, or other elements to rescue a hero, collect treasure, defeat monsters, and save a princess.

How To Play

Use your imagination to arrange the puzzle pipes and elbows to create a perfect connection between the starting point and the target. Consider the properties of different liquids to defeat enemies and rescue the hero and princess. Collect treasures as you solve the puzzles.
